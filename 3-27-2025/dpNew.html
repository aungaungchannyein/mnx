Withdrawal

Withdrawal Priority Procedure

Monaxa works hard to protect against fraud and money laundering using this simple and clear policy.

All withdrawal/refunds go back to the originating source of funds first.
You cannot deposit from one method and withdrawal to another until the original amount deposited is complete.
Example:
Made a deposit via Bank Card?
Your withdrawn funds will go back to the original depositing method in the original currency. Until it exceeds your original deposit amount. If exceeded, the remaining funds will be processed via your choice as an E-wallet, Crypto wallet, Local Bank Transfer, Local Deposit or International Bank transfer.
