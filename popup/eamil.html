In an effort to increase our alignment with regulatory institutions, we will be making some changes that will affect your account.

Effective immediately:

Monaxa will no longer be serving clients who are residents of Spain, and all accounts from residents of Spain will be closed by December 16th, 2024.

Key Actions Required:

  • Close Open Positions:
    You must close all open trading positions within 7 days of this notification.
  • Withdraw Funds:
    All funds must be withdrawn to the original source of deposit.
  • Account Closure:
    Your account profile will be permanently closed once funds are fully withdrawn.

Any positions not closed by December 16th, 2024, will be closed manually by Monaxa.

Important Notices:

- Failure to take action within the specified timeframe may result in automatic position closure and fund liquidation.

- We recommend logging into your trading account immediately to manage your positions and withdrawals.

If you have any questions or require assistance, please contact our Monaxa Assist team.
