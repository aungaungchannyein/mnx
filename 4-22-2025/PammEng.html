Be A PAMM Investor
Be A PAMM Manager
 
 

Income

Without limitations as long as the PAMM Manager’s trade strategy works.

Certainty

The PAMM Manager can trade your funds but cannot withdraw your funds.

 
 

Flexibility

Invest into many PAMM Managers and withdraw profit/funds at any time.

Transparency

Trade history, and account performance is easily viewed in the client portal.



 
 

How It Works

You invest all or some of your capital into a PAMM Managers strategy to earn a proportional share of the profits.The PAMM Manager earns a portion of the profits based on their investment contribution plus commissions from profitable activity. ONLY profitable activity. It’s a win/win situation.

(Please Note: Even though you can deposit and withdraw at any time, all deposits and withdrawals will take place automatically during rollover @ 00:00 (Midnight), Timezone: UTC.)

 

See the diagram below:



 

*All profits and losses are proportionally distributed to the invested funds.
**PAMM Managers earn commission as a percentage of the investor’s profit. In this example, it’s 20%.



   
 

It's So Easy

Open an account with Monaxa (if you haven’t already).

Login to the Monaxa Portal and click the PAMM Accounts Tab on the lower left side of your screen.

Click the Research Managers tab, do a deep dive and select one of many managers to invest in.

Follow the prompts, invest funds and keep an eye on your investments by clicking the My Investments tab.

It's So Easy

Any client of ours can be a PAMM Manager. But to get investors you need to have demonstrated trade activity within the PAMM system and you need to have great profitability.
